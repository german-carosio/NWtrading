About us

Mision

We are agricultural specialties trading company based in Miami, Florida. We provide a comprehensive solution to the supply needs of our clients. We offer a global network of producers and processors, the advice and information necessary to help our customers make the right purchasing decision. More than a supplier, we consider ourselves a strategic partner of your business. Agriculture and markets are our passions, which came together to form New World Trading.

Vision

To be the most competitive and comprehensive option for the specialty commercial chain, increasing the profitability of the whole in all the markets we operate in.

Values

Integrity, transparency, punctuality, cooperativism
